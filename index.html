About me

A quick overview of who I am and how I like to work.

My creative approach

I’m a self-driven designer who started by creating graphics for friends and streamers and turned it into ongoing freelance work. I love exploring colour, typography and composition while still keeping everything clean and easy to understand.

Whether it’s a gaming logo, a YouTube thumbnail or a motion piece in After Effects, my goal is always the same: strong focus, clear messaging and visuals that feel balanced on both desktop and mobile screens.

Strengths

Colour & lighting sense
Fast learner & problem solver
Clean, readable layouts
Timing & beat-synced motion
High attention to detail
Creator-focused design mindset

My journey

I first got into design by making thumbnails, banners and logos for friends. From there, I started experimenting with more advanced Photoshop work and then moved into motion graphics, creating intros, outros and animated text for gaming content.

Right now I’m studying Game Development at MOGO Media Academy, which helps me understand how visuals support gameplay, pacing and overall experience. I’m excited to grow in a professional CG / design environment, work with real production teams and keep pushing my work to a higher level.
